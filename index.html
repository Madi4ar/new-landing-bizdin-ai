FAQ

What does bizdin.ai do differently from other video analytics tools?

Unlike traditional tools that just detect objects, bizdin.ai understands what’s happening. It turns any camera into a smart system that sees
context—whether it’s spotting a fight, analyzing customer behavior, or preventing incidents—all in real time, without relying on the cloud.

How does bizdin.ai work with existing cameras?

We don’t sell hardware—we supercharge what you already have. bizdin.ai connects to any IP or RTSP camera, processing video locally on-site. No need to replace equipment, just plug in and let our AI do the rest.

Can bizdin.ai really prevent incidents like fights or fires?

Yes, it’s built for that. Our AI analyzes live feeds to detect threats—like fights, weapons, or smoke—instantly, then triggers alerts or actions (like notifying security) to stop trouble before it escalates.

Why doesn’t bizdin.ai need cloud processing like other AI systems?

We use Edge AI, meaning all the heavy lifting happens locally. This cuts delays, keeps your data secure, and works even without internet—perfect for real-time needs like safety or business ops.

Who can use bizdin.ai?

Anyone with cameras and a need for insights! Think schools tracking safety, businesses optimizing customer flow, or cities managing security. From small shops to governments, we’re built to scale.

Do I need coding skills to set up bizdin.ai?

Nope, it’s no-code all the way. Just type a command like ‘Alert me if a fight starts,’ and our AI sets it up. You get powerful customization without touching a line of code.
